Lemon Herb Khichdi

⏱ 30 min  🔥 320 kcal   🥩 18g protein 18g protein per serving, mostly from moong dal   🍽 2 servings  Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup moong dal
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Fresh coriander

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and dal together.
  2. Pressure cook with turmeric and salt for 3 whistles.
  3. Stir in ghee, lemon juice, and coriander before serving.
